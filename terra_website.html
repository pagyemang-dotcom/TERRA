The Four Layers

Address as Physics

A postal address is a human convention — it changes with governments, borders, and infrastructure. A Unified Physical Address encodes the same location using invariants that existed before humanity and will exist long after.

LAYER 01
⚡
Gravitational
G-addr = Φ(P) − W₀
W₀ = 62,636,853.4 m²s⁻²
(IAG 2015 geoid reference)

Einstein's equivalence principle: a clock at position P runs at a rate uniquely determined by the local gravitational potential. Every metre of altitude changes G-addr by ~9.8 m²s⁻².

Precision: ±0.3 m today → ±1 cm (2028)
LAYER 02
✦
Pulsar
Δtₖ = −(r_P · n̂ₖ)/c
B1937+21, J0437−4715,
J1713+0747

Millisecond pulsars are the most stable natural clocks known. The Roemer delay residuals encode your 3D position in the Solar System. Works on Mars. Works at 100 AU. GPS does not.

Precision: ±30 m today → ±1 cm (2030)
LAYER 03
⬢
Isotopic
I = {⁷Sr/⁶Sr, δ¹⁸O,
²⁰⁶Pb/²⁰⁷Pb, δ¹³C, δ¹⁵N}
IAEA · GEOROC · ISCN

4.5 billion years of plate tectonics and precipitation cycles have written a unique geochemical fingerprint into the soil, water, and rock of every location on Earth. Cannot be spoofed.

Precision: ±50 km today → ±1 km (2030)
LAYER 04
◯

All three physical layers vary slightly over time. The timestamp anchors the UPA to a specific epoch, making it a complete spacetime coordinate rather than merely a spatial one.

Precision: ±1 second

The Problem

4 Billion People.
No Address.

01

No Infrastructure Required

A postal address requires roads, post offices, and mapping agencies. TERRA requires one smartphone with GPS and a barometer. The address computes from physics, not from governance.

02

Impossible to Forge

The gravitational potential at your location is determined by the distribution of mass in the Earth's mantle. It cannot be spoofed. No government can reassign it. No war can erase it.

03

Works Beyond GPS

The pulsar timing layer works anywhere in the Solar System. Mars has a UPA. A point 100 AU from the Sun has a UPA. No existing address system functions beyond Earth orbit.

04

Permanent Record

The isotopic signature records 4.5 billion years of Earth chemistry. A UPA from 2026 can be verified in 3026 by measuring the same isotope ratios. No human convention lasts this long.

05

Financial Inclusion

The AXIOM MIS uses TERRA for creditworthiness scores for the unbanked. Location stability encoded in physics replaces postal records that billions of people do not have.

06

Open and Free

TERRA is fully open source. The physics is published. The code is on GitHub. The REST API is free to use. The address system belongs to no company and no nation.

REST API

The TERRA API runs on Render.com. It accepts GPS coordinates and returns a complete Unified Physical Address. Free to use. No API key required.
